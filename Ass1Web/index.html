Introduction

Automobili Lamborghini S.p.A.is an Italian brand and manufacturer of luxury sports cars and SUVs based in Sant'Agata Bolognese. The company is owned by the Volkswagen Group through its subsidiary Audi.

Lamborghini

  • Location : Headquarters and factory: Sant'Agata, Bolognese, near Modena.
  • Country : Italia
  • Parent : Volkswagen group (via Audi)

Lamborghini is the only sport car brand can rival the prestige status of Ferrari. In many ways, it is even more exotic than Ferrari in the eyes of car lovers, thanks to the loss-making supercars it produced throughout its 40 years history - Miura, Countach, Diablo and Murcielago.
